Fall, "Forward". 넘어지세요, "앞으로"

Posted on January 1, 2023 by Dayoung Jeong
Motivation Speech

Denzel Washington is a renowned actor and filmmaker who has delivered many inspiring speeches in various occasions. Some of his most famous speeches are:

His commencement speech at the University of Pennsylvania in 2011, where he encouraged the graduates to “fall forward” and learn from their failures.

His motivational speech at the NAACP Image Awards in 2017, where he urged the audience to “keep working” and never give up on their dreams.

Denzel Washington`s speeches are full of wisdom, humor, and passion.

He often shares his personal stories and experiences to illustrate his points and connect with his listeners. He also challenges his listeners to think critically, act courageously, and live authentically.
